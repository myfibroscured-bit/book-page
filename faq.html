Is fibromyalgia really reversible?

Conventional medicine often describes fibromyalgia as a lifelong, incurable condition. Lindsey's own experience — going from housebound and on over 60 tablets a day to medication-free and symptom-free — suggests genuine, lasting change is possible for some people through a holistic, mindset-led approach. Individual results vary, and this isn't a guarantee or medical claim.

Do I need to stop my medication to work with you?

No. Never stop or change prescribed medication without talking to your doctor first. Coaching focuses on mindset, lifestyle, and holistic strategies alongside whatever medical care you're already receiving.

Are you a doctor?

No. Lindsey is a fibromyalgia recovery coach speaking from lived experience, not a licensed medical professional. Coaching is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment — see the Medical Disclaimer for details.

What's the difference between the book, the community, and the 12-Week Experience?

The book, From Fibromyalgia to Freedom, is a self-paced roadmap you can read anytime. The Community is ongoing monthly support with live sessions and workshops. The 12-Week Experience is the full guided group coaching program with live support, recordings, and lifetime community access. See Work With Me for full details and pricing.

I'm not ready to commit to anything — where should I start?

Start with the free Quick Start Guide, or book a free 15-minute Clarity Call. Both are no-pressure ways to see if this approach resonates with you.

Is there a podcast or video content yet?

A podcast is in the works — see the Podcast page. In the meantime, follow along on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook, and join the free Facebook community for ongoing content.
