From Diagnosis to Freedom. This isn't just a story — it's a roadmap.

What if fibromyalgia wasn't a life sentence? At my worst, I was housebound, taking over 60 tablets a day just to get through. I lived in survival mode — but something in me knew there had to be more to life than just managing symptoms.

This book is the journey of how I proved that inner whisper right. It isn't about miracles — it's about reclaiming your power. I share the exact mindset shifts, lifestyle changes, and tools that guided me back to my most powerful self. The roadmap I wish I'd had — practical, grounded, and rooted in lived experience.
