This Isn't Theory. I Lived It.

From housebound on 60+ tablets a day to free

At my worst, I was housebound for nearly a year, juggling three young children, and taking over 60 tablets a day just to get through. Sitting on the floor meant crawling to a chair to pull myself up. I felt like an elderly person trapped in a young body.

Doctors prescribed medication after medication, each with its own side effects, leading to more prescriptions to counteract those. I knew I had to break the cycle. Through real, sustained changes — mindset, nervous system, movement, nutrition, self-care — I discovered medication was the last thing my body needed.

Today I'm no longer surviving. I'm living — medication-free, grounded, and free, working as a digital nomad. This program is everything that got me there, distilled into 12 weeks.
